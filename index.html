About Bit by Bit

Our Aims

  • Pursue or inspire an interest in computer science
  • Make coding projects together, or individually, to expand knowledge of computer science
  • Teach Computer Science as we code along, taking in people who may not have as much experience than others in coding.
  • Have fun and mix our own individual interests with computer science

Club Activites

  • Individual Projects that we can help with for individual desires
  • Group Projects where we work on a larger-scale project together
  • Hackathons, competing in coding competitions.
  • Teaching people who have little to no experience with coding how to code.
